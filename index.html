Features

The Shamrock Agriculture System is designed to help farmers make better day-to-day decisions. It provides smart crop suggestions based on soil and weather conditions, real-time weather updates, and crop planning guidance.

It’s also useful for urban users who want to grow their own plants at home or on terraces — making farming accessible for everyone.
